Hello! I will be biking from Rockport to Provincetown starting September 28th. It will be a 3 day ride totaling about 200 miles! I'm doing this ride as a fundraiser for an organization I co-founded, the Yoga Diversity Initiative (YDI). YDI's mission is to make the practice of yoga more accessible to People of Color in the Boston area. One of the barriers is the very low number of yoga teachers that are People of Color. YDI is working to address this by providing scholarships for yoga teacher trainings to People of Color.

YDI formed 2 years ago and we have awarded three scholarships so far. This year we would like to award 2 scholarships so I'm trying to raise $5000. It's been exciting to see our program grow and I've heard so many people in the Boston yoga community say "Yes! We need this. Thank-you for doing this work"

Donations to the ride are tax deductable. To learn more about YDI visit our website. Thanks for your support!
